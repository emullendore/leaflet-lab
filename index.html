Sulfur Dioxide Pollution

Sulfur Dioxide Pollutants in the Top 15 Most Polluted Cities in 2015 (1990-2014)

Enacted by the EPA, the 1963 Clean Air Act set national ambient air quality standards. Sulfur dioxideis one of six major criteria pollutants. The largest source of SO2 comes from fossil fuel combustion at power plants (ie. coal-fired power plants). In 1990, the Clean Air Act was amended to directly address sulfur dioxide's impacts. SO2 contributes to acid rain as well as respiratory illness in people. While SO2 has been cut by about two thirds since 1990s levels, there is still irreperable damage to some ecosystem.
